Getting Around

Both the ceremony and reception venues are in relatively urban areas, so the easiest ways to get around are typically the MBTA (Hope Fellowship Church is close to the Red Line, and Ming's Seafood Restaurant is close to the Orange Line) and Uber and Lyft.

Looking for a ride?

If you'd prefer to carpool with others, you can indicate that on your RSVP. If you forgot to note that on your RSVP, just let us know!

Ceremony Parking

There is a small parking lot next to the church. Once that fills up, you may park along Orchard St and Beech St (you'll see Permit Parking Only signs on those streets, but we made an arrangement with the city of Cambridge to avoid ticketing around the church during our wedding. If those streets are full or you would prefer to park elsewhere, there is 2 hour free parking along Elm St.

Reception Parking

Ming's has a parking lot! If that fills up (though it's unlikely), there are also a few parking garages nearby.
