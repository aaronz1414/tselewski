Boston

Getting to Boston

You can fly into Boston through Logan Airport, or there are a number of Amtrak and bus routes that stop at South Station in Boston

Things to do in Boston

  • Walk The Freedom Trail to see historical sites in Boston.
  • Visit Boston Common and Boston Public Garden
  • Visit MIT and Harvard
  • Walk along the Esplanade
  • Visit the Aquarium
  • Visit the Museum of Fine Arts (MFA)
  • Visit the Boston Harbor Islands
