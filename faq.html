When should I RSVP by?
Please RSVP before September 1, 2019 via our RSVP page!
Do I get a plus one?
When you RSVP, it will indicate to you if you have a plus one or not. If you have any questions, feel free to ask us!
How do I get to Boston?
You can fly into Boston through Logan Airport, or there are a number of Amtrak and bus routes that stop at South Station in Boston.
Where should I stay?
Details about our hotel block and other accommodations can be found on our Accommodations Page.
How do I get around?
Both the ceremony and reception venues are in relatively urban areas, so the easiest ways to get around are typically the MBTA (Hope Fellowship Church is close to the Red Line, and Ming's Seafood Restaurant is close to the Orange Line) and Uber and Lyft.
Where can I park during the ceremony?
You may park along Orchard St and Beech St. You'll see Permit Parking Only signs on those streets, but we made an arrangement with the city of Cambridge to avoid ticketing around the church during our wedding. If you do happen to be incorrectly given a parking ticket, let us know and we'll clarify with the city.

If those streets are full or you would prefer to park elsewhere, there is 2 hour free parking along Elm St.
Where can I park during the reception?
Ming's has a parking lot behind the restaurant! If that fills up (though it's unlikely), there are also a few parking garages nearby.
What if I'm looking for people to travel to Boston with?
If you're looking for people to travel with, you can indicate that on your RSVP. We will try to connect you with others in your area. If you forgot to note that on your RSVP, just let us know!
What should I wear?
Semi-formal attire is requested.
