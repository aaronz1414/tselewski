Hotel Wedding Block

Location
  • AC Hotel Boston Marriott North
  • 95 Station Landing, Medford, MA 02155
  • 13 minute drive from ceremony venue
  • 8 minute drive from reception venue
Dates
Rooms in our wedding block can be booked Thursday through Saturday night (10/10 - 10/12), so that you can come early to explore Boston if you'd like.
Reservations
  • Reserve through our wedding block here
  • We have a limited number of guaranteed rooms in our wedding block, so it will be first come, first served.
  • Reservation deadline: Tuesday, September 10, 2019

Once our wedding block fills up we're allowed to request extra rooms to be added at our wedding block rate. So if you'd like to reserve through our wedding block but the wedding block seems to be filled, let us know and we'll try to get extra rooms added - we just can't guarantee that we'll be able to add the extra rooms at the same rate. The sooner in advance you book the better. The earlier our wedding block fills up the more likely it is that we can get extra rooms at the same rate.
